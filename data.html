💡 Success Tips

🎯 Consistency is Key

Code every single day, even if just 30 minutes. Small daily progress beats intense weekend marathons.

🔍 Quality over Quantity

Understand solutions deeply rather than rushing through problems. Focus on patterns and concepts.

⏰ Time Management

Practice with timer from Month 3 onwards. Simulate real interview conditions.

🤝 Mock Interviews

Start mock interviews from Month 4. Practice explaining your thought process clearly.
