Develop the mindset that separates masters from beginners:

  • Always ask "Why?" - understand the business problem before jumping to solutions
  • Think in systems - consider how your code affects the entire application ecosystem
  • Embrace trade-offs - every technical decision has costs and benefits
  • Measure everything - use data to drive technical decisions
  • Plan for failure - assume things will break and design accordingly
  • Optimize for maintainability - future developers will thank you
  • Stay curious - continuously learn and challenge your assumptions
